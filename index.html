The story

A studio, just getting started.

Everyday Studio is one person, building small apps for everyday life. Most software is built for big moments — we build for the small ones: what to eat, where to take the kids, what to cook with what's in the fridge, where to find a quiet hour to work.

This is day one. The first app, Cheap Eats, launches soon. After that, one new app at a time — each one focused on a single everyday problem, made to be picked up in 30 seconds and put down just as fast.

No VC. No tracking. No 12-step onboarding. Just useful apps, made by a human. If that sounds like your kind of software, you're in the right place.
